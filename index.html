Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of single rooms are generally between 37 m^2 to 45 m^2.

Family Room

A parlour or living room connected with to one or more bedrooms. (A room with one or more bedrooms and a separate living space.) The room size or area of suite rooms are generally between 70 m^2 to 100 m^2.

Duplex Room

The most expensive room provided by a hotel. Usually, only one suplex suite is available is one single hotel property. Similar to the normal suites, a Duplex suite always has one or more bedrooms and a living space with a string emphasis on grand in-room decoration, high-quality amenities and supplies, and tailor-made services (e.g.personal butler during the stay).
